Hey you,

I'm not great with words — you know that. But today felt like a day where I should at least try, because you deserve to hear it.

I don't think you realize how much you've changed things for me. Not in some dramatic, movie-script kind of way — just quietly. The way you listen. The way you show up. The way you make even the ordinary stuff feel like it actually matters. That's rare, and I never want to take it for granted.

You are more than I expected, more than I probably deserve, and honestly more than I know how to say thank you for. But I'm trying.

I'm grateful you exist. Not in some generic, greeting-card way — I mean I'm genuinely grateful that you were born, that our paths crossed, that I get to know you. The world is better because you're in it, and my world especially.

I hope this year gives you everything you've been quietly hoping for. The things you don't say out loud, the dreams you keep to yourself — I hope they all find their way to you.

— yours
